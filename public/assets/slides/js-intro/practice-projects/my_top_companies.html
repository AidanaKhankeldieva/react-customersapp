Question

Create an array to hold your top companies where you would like to work.

For each choice log to the screen a string like:

"My 1st choice is Microsoft", "My 2nd choice is Amazon".

Pick the right suffix for the number based on what it is.

Step 3

Step 5

Iterate your array inside the function

Step 6

Step 7

Step 8
